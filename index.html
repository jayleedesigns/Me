Hello I'm Jason Nester

I am a hardworking guy with a comprehensive knowledge of web and multimedia technologies from Northeast Ohio. I like to create colorful and dynamic media that engages and entertains. I have over 14 years experience in web design and over 18 in art and traditional illustration. I have worked at several big creative agencies as well as Kent State University as An Educational Technologies Designer. I have a wide skill set including but not limited to, graphic design, illustration, character design, branding, motion graphics and web design and development, as well as mobile app and game development. I am looking for opportunities in Graphic Design and Front-End Development.

I specialize in

  • Developing Concepts

    Every great design starts with an idea/concept. An idea is what forms the fundamental parts. It’s very important that all the fundamental parts are well defined and working.

  • Visualizing Ideas

    The best way to present an idea is to visualise it. It makes your idea alive. In many cases, visualisation helps users understand the story that a business wants to tell them.

  • Designing Interfaces

    My main area of expertise is UI design. Nowadays, interface is a very important part of the online product. The right UI will guarantee your success.

  • Developing Products

    Developing products is my passion. It’s more than just design. It’s about taking that design to the next level and developing the Front-End Experience.
